Winter clothing drive 2022

Path of Hope kicked off the holiday season by organizing a winter clothing drive.

March 6, 2022

Path of Hope kicked off the holiday season by organizing a winter clothing drive in partnership with a local school district. Over the course of several weeks, we collected books, toys, and warm winter clothing from three school campuses, with the goal of providing support to those in need during the colder months.

Thanks to the generosity of our partners and supporters, we were able to gather more than 45 boxes of donated items, which included coats, hats, gloves, and other essential winter gear. These donations were then distributed to families and individuals in the community who were experiencing hardship, providing warmth, comfort, and a sense of hope during the holiday season.
