Ramadan Food Drive 2022

Path of Hope organized a fun and engaging sports event in partnership with a local basketball academy.

April 7, 2022

Path of Hope organized a successful food drive during the holy month of Ramadan, in order to support those in need and give back to our community. Working in partnership with local charities, we collected a wide variety of food items, including canned goods, dried fruits, and other non-perishable items.

Thanks to the generosity of our supporters and volunteers, we were able to gather a large amount of food, which we then distributed to various charities across the region. These donations helped to ensure that families in need had access to nutritious and wholesome meals during this special time of year.

The food drive was not only a success in terms of the amount of food collected, but also in terms of the sense of community and camaraderie that it fostered. Volunteers from diverse backgrounds came together to work towards a common goal, demonstrating the power of unity and compassion.
