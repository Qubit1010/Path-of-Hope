Tablets for Transformation 2023

Building a Brighter Future for All

June 6, 2023
Riyadh

Path of Hope is excited to announce our upcoming event, which aims to provide educational opportunities to those in need and help them prosper in their personal and professional lives. In partnership with a local charity group, we will be hosting a series of workshops on a variety of topics, taught by dedicated volunteers who are passionate about sharing their knowledge and skills.

At the event, attendees will have the opportunity to learn about a wide range of subjects, from financial literacy and entrepreneurship to language learning and computer skills. Each workshop will be led by a skilled volunteer who will provide practical and valuable instruction to help attendees build skills and knowledge that they can use to improve their lives.

In addition, we will be raising funds to purchase tablets, which will be distributed to attendees to aid in their continued learning and growth. Our goal is to empower individuals who may not have had access to these resources before, and help them pave their own path towards a brighter future.

Join us in our mission to bridge the educational gap and create a more equitable society for all!
