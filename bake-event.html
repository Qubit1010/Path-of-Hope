Bake Sale 2022

Path of Hope organized a highly successful bake sale to raise funds for our programs and services.

2022/7/5
John Doe

Path of Hope organized a highly successful bake sale to raise funds for our programs and services, in partnership with a group of dedicated student volunteers. Over the course of several weeks, our volunteers worked tirelessly to bake and prepare an array of delicious treats, including cookies, cupcakes, brownies, and more.

Thanks to the hard work of our volunteers and the generosity of our supporters, we were able to raise an impressive 11,000 riyals in just a few hours.

This event not only provided much-needed funding for our organization but also provided an opportunity for our volunteers to develop their skills, connect with their community, and make a meaningful impact in the lives of others. By coming together and working towards a common goal, we were able to achieve great success and create a positive change in our community.
