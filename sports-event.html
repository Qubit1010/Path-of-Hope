Sports event 2022

Path of Hope organized a fun and engaging sports event in partnership with a local basketball academy. The event featured over 100 attendees, including players, spectators, and supporters, who came together to enjoy a day of basketball and community building.

At the event, we divided players into teams representing different charity groups, giving them the opportunity to compete for a good cause. Spectators also had the chance to participate in fun activities and games, with prizes and giveaways provided by our sponsors.

Adding to the festive atmosphere, we also brought in a food truck to provide tasty snacks and refreshments for all attendees. Thanks to the generosity of our sponsors and supporters, we were able to raise around 10,000 riyals in funds, which will go towards the charities represented.

This event not only provided a fun and exciting experience for all involved but also helped to promote our organization's mission and values in the community. By creating opportunities for people to come together and make a positive impact, we are paving the way for a brighter future for all.
